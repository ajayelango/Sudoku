Privacy Policy - Sudoku App

Effective Date: April 2026

Thank you for using our Sudoku app (“the App”). Your privacy is extremely important to us. This Privacy Policy explains in detail how we handle user information, what data (if any) is collected, and your rights as a user.

By using the App, you agree to the terms outlined in this Privacy Policy.

1. Overview

Our App is designed to provide a simple, distraction-free Sudoku experience. We prioritize user privacy and have intentionally built the App to operate without collecting personal data.

2. Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, transmit, or process any personal or sensitive user data.

This includes, but is not limited to:

  • No names, email addresses, or contact information
  • No location data
  • No device identifiers
  • No usage tracking or analytics
  • No gameplay behavior tracking stored externally

All gameplay (such as puzzle progress, scores, or settings) is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to any server.

3. Local Data Storage

The App may store limited data locally on your device to enhance user experience. This may include:

  • Game progress (current puzzle state)
  • User preferences (e.g., difficulty level, settings)
  • Walkthrough/tutorial completion status

This data remains entirely on your device and can be removed by uninstalling the App or clearing the app data through your device settings.

4. Children's Privacy

The App is suitable for users of all ages, including children under the age of 13. We comply with applicable child privacy protection laws, including the Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

Since we do not collect any personal information, we do not knowingly collect data from children or any other users.

5. Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate any third-party services that collect or process user data.

This means:

  • No analytics SDKs (e.g., Firebase Analytics)
  • No advertising networks
  • No tracking tools
  • No external APIs that collect personal data

If third-party services are added in future updates, this Privacy Policy will be updated accordingly with full transparency.

6. Data Security

Because we do not collect or transmit user data, there is no risk of your personal information being exposed through our systems.

Any data stored locally on your device is protected by your device’s own security mechanisms.

7. User Rights

Since we do not collect or process personal data, there is no personal data to access, modify, or delete from our servers.

However, you retain full control over your local data and may:

  • Clear app data through device settings
  • Uninstall the App at any time

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in the App, legal requirements, or best practices.

When updates are made:

  • The “Effective Date” will be revised
  • Changes will be clearly reflected on this page

Continued use of the App after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Compliance with Laws

This Privacy Policy is designed to comply with applicable privacy laws and regulations, including but not limited to:

  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)
  • Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA)

As no personal data is collected, compliance requirements are minimal.
